Rules

You may not attempt to break other's props with your own. (proppushing)


Trapping/blocking players, other's props or builds is not allowed. (propblocking)


Spawning hostile NPCs near spawn or inside other's bases is not allowed.


You may propkill only with props around playersize or less. (breendesk, locker, etc.)


Don't spam; this includes explosives like satchels, NPCs, props, and so on.


If you're constantly being annoying, don't complain when others return the favour.

Discord Info

Commands (#game-chat):

!status, !staff, !players, !sync

Important channels:

#reports - strictly for making reports about rule breaking.
Please use a format following [Name/SteamID], did [rule broken], here is [screenshot].
